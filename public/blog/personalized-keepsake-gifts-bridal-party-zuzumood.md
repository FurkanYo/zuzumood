## FAQ

### What makes a gift a 'keepsake'?
A keepsake gift is an item intended to be kept and treasured for a long time, often because it holds sentimental value or commemorates a special occasion. Unlike disposable items, keepsakes are typically made with quality materials and design, meant to endure and evoke cherished memories over the years.

### How can I personalize gifts without them feeling generic or 'cheesy'?
To avoid generic personalization, focus on subtle, meaningful touches. Instead of overtly themed items, choose minimalist designs with clean typography for initials, dates, or short, heartfelt messages.
