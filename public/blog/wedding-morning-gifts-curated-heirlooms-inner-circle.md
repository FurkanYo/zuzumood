## Curated Heirlooms for a Memorable Wedding Morning

1. **Monogrammed Silk Robes** — Luxurious silk robes, subtly embroidered with initials, offer comfort and elegance for getting-ready photos. A true quiet luxury staple.
2. **Personalized Keepsake Jewelry** — Delicate necklaces or bracelets with a birthstone, initial, or a small, meaningful charm. Timeless pieces they can wear for years.
3. **Engraved Compact Mirrors** — A chic, minimalist compact mirror, engraved with the wedding date or a personal message, perfect for touch-ups throughout the day.
4. **Cashmere Eye Masks** — For a moment of serene relaxation amidst the excitement, a soft cashmere eye mask is a thoughtful, luxurious gesture.
5. **Customized Canvas Totes** — High-quality, minimalist canvas totes, personalized with a name or role, ideal for carrying essentials on the wedding day and beyond.
6. **Heirloom Scented Candles** — A beautifully crafted candle with a sophisticated, calming scent, presented in a reusable vessel, creating a lasting sensory memory.
7. **Elegant Hair Accessories** — Pearl or crystal hairpins, or a delicate silk scrunchie, to complement their wedding day look and serve as a beautiful memento.
8. **Bespoke Stationery Sets** — A small, personalized stationery set with elegant notecards, encouraging them to pen their own thoughts and memories.
9. **Premium Hydration Bottles** — Sleek, insulated water bottles in a minimalist design, ensuring they stay refreshed in style throughout the celebratory day.
10. **Framed Sentimental Photo** — A small, elegant frame holding a cherished photo of you and the recipient, a deeply personal and sentimental keepsake.
11. **Luxury Lip Balm & Hand Cream Set** — A small, curated set of premium, unscented lip balm and hand cream for subtle self-care during the busy morning.
12. **Personalized Silk Sleep Masks** — A refined silk sleep mask, perhaps with a subtle monogram, for a restful night before or a moment of calm during the day.
