## Budget Guide

Quiet luxury doesn't necessarily mean exorbitant prices, but rather a focus on value, quality, and intentionality. Instead of a large box filled with many small, generic items, consider one or two premium, well-chosen pieces. For a truly elevated ask, allocate a budget that allows for superior materials and craftsmanship. Remember, the goal is to convey appreciation through thoughtful selection, not sheer expense. A single, beautifully personalized item often makes a more profound impact than a collection of lesser quality goods.
