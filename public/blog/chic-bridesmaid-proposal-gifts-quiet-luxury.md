## Budget Guide

Quiet luxury doesn't necessarily mean extravagant spending, but rather a focus on quality over quantity. For bridesmaid proposal gifts, consider a budget range of $50-$150 per person. This allows for selecting premium, thoughtful items that feel substantial without breaking the bank. Prioritize one or two truly exceptional pieces over a box filled with many less impactful items. Remember, the sentiment and the quality of the gesture are paramount. Investing in a personalized, heirloom-quality item ensures it will be cherished far beyond the wedding day.
